SiteHQ Chat Widget

A standalone, embeddable chat widget for your website

Getting Started

Integrate the SiteHQ chat widget into your website with just a single line of code:

<script src="https://your-website.com/standalone-widget.js" data-sitehq-auto="true" data-api-key="YOUR_API_KEY" data-agent-id="YOUR_AGENT_ID"></script>

Custom Integration

For more control over the widget placement and initialization:

<!-- Step 1: Include the script --> <script src="https://your-website.com/standalone-widget.js"></script> <!-- Step 2: Add the custom element anywhere on your page --> <sitehq-chat-widget api-key="YOUR_API_KEY" agent-id="YOUR_AGENT_ID" theme='{"primary":"#5c078c","background":"#ffffff","text":"#333333"}' dark-mode="false"> </sitehq-chat-widget>

Features

Dark Mode Support

Built-in dark mode toggle for optimal user experience in any lighting condition.

Customizable Theme

Match your brand with custom colors for primary, background, and text elements.

Voice Capabilities

Support for voice input and output through the integrated microphone interface.

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices with adaptive layout.

Visual Effects

Engaging animations and visual effects that enhance user experience.

Shadow DOM Isolation

Widget styles won't conflict with your existing website styles.
